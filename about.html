What it is

What is Solar Pre-Qualification?

Solar pre-qualification is different from a quote or a sales pitch. It's a data-driven assessment of your home's solar potential, generated entirely from satellite and utility data — no site visit, no salesperson.

You get a personalised report showing your estimated annual savings in dollars. What you do with that information is entirely up to you.

  • ✓ Generated from your roof's actual data — not an average
  • ✓ Results in 60 seconds, emailed directly to you
  • ✓ No sales call scheduled automatically
  • ✓ Not a binding quote — just real information
📋

Pre-Qualification vs Quote

Pre-Qualification: Fast, data-driven estimate of your savings potential. No commitment. No visit. Yours to keep.

Quote: Requires a site inspection and a conversation with a sales team. A later step, only if you want to proceed.

The Platform

How the SolarSight Platform Works

SolarSight combines three best-in-class data sources to build a pre-qualification that reflects your home's specific situation.

🛰

Google Solar API

Provides precise roof geometry, orientation, tilt angle, and annual solar exposure hours for every section of your roof.

🗺

Nearmap Aerial Imagery

High-resolution aerial imagery updated more frequently than Google Maps — used to verify roof condition, obstructions and shading.

⚡

EIA Utility Rate Data

Live utility rate data from the US Energy Information Administration — so your savings estimate reflects what you actually pay for electricity in DC or Maryland.
