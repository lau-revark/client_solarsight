Washington DC's solar pre-qualification platform. Real data. Real savings. No pressure.

SolarSight is a free pre-qualification service for property owners in Washington, DC. Savings figures and system estimates are generated from publicly available data — Google Solar API, Nearmap aerial imagery, and EIA Form 861 — together with the address you submit. They are good-faith estimates, not binding quotes, financing offers, or guarantees of solar suitability or performance. A site-specific design and formal proposal from a licensed solar contractor is required before any installation. SolarSight is operated by Phoenix Solar Group.
