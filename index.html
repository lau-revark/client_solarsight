Built on Data You Can Trust

The Same Data Used by Leading Solar Companies

No guesswork. No assumptions. SolarSight's pre-qualification is built on the same data infrastructure used by solar industry professionals and government agencies.

Google Solar API

Roof assessment and solar irradiance data from Google's satellite and AI analysis of your specific property. Factors in roof size, pitch, orientation, shading, and local irradiance patterns.

Nearmap Aerial Imagery

High-resolution aerial imagery updated regularly across the US. SolarSight uses this to validate and refine roof geometry data — ensuring your pre-qualification reflects your actual property.

EIA Form 861 Data

Real utility and market data from the US Energy Information Administration. Your savings estimate is based on the real cost of electricity in your specific area, not a national average.

Mapbox Geocoding

Address autocomplete and geocoding from Mapbox — used to validate every property at the front door so your pre-qualification is tied to the exact parcel you live on, not a near-miss.
